Dog Massive is an e-commerce platform that deals in the sale of dogs, dog foods, dog accessories and provision of dog grooming and veterinary services to dog enthusiasts. Dogs are more than pets to us and for that reason, we are making it easier for people to own dogs. At dog massive, one can get a variety of services that aim at making the lives of their dogs smooth. Our services are divided into the following categories:

  1. Sale of Dogs

    2. We deal in the sale of different breeds of dogs of different sizes and ages. Breeders, having met our quality and standards policy sign up with us. Dog Massive gives them a platform to sell their dogs to numerous dog enthusiasts. We therefore connect our breeders to potential clients and our highly esteemed clients to their dream dogs.

  2. Veterinary services

    3. We have a team of highly skilled veterinary officers who are always on standby to assist our clients as per their requests.   3. Sale of Dog Foods and dog accessories

    4. Dog massive deals in the sale of dog foods and all dog accessories that our clients may be in need of. Clients can order of dog foods and any dog accessories that have been listed for sale in our website. Clients simply make orders that shall be delivered to a location of their choice within shortest time possible.

  4. Dog Grooming Services

    5. We have a team of highly skilled and trained experts who will offer you al the dog grooming services that you need. Clients in need of this service simply make a booking or a request with us.
